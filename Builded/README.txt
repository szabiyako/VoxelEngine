Controls:
---------
• Move the mouse to change the camera angle;
• WASD                     – move camera;
• Space                    - move camera up;
• Ctrl                     - move camera down;
• R                        - enable/disable mouse cursor;
• F                        - fullsize/minimise window;
• Alt+Enter                – enable/disable fullscreen mode;
• CapsLock                 - enable/disable high speed mode;
• Shift                    - pressing increases speed;
• Q                        - enable/disable multiplyiing voxels;
• E                        - place light source in camera position;
• C                        - change light source intensity;
• T                        - attach/detach the light to the camera;
• V                        – recreate the world.

Files:
------
save.txt                   - stores the seed of the world, camera coordinates and angles and the coordinates of the light source;
distance.txt               - stores render distance (in chunks);
changes.data               - keeps all the changes of the world
